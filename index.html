A science fiction thriller

RAIN

Black Mirror meets Fahrenheit 451

Devon Rosefeld's brain has been hijacked — by a grandson who doesn't exist yet.

A static-filled voice from thirty years in the future delivers a chilling warning: tech mogul Peyton Viallis, CEO of RAIN, is about to pivot from Silicon Valley darling to presidential candidate. And when she wins, her revolutionary neural chip will give her a grip on the American mind that no election can undo.

After a bourbon-soaked first date with Ada, Devon unknowingly tips the first domino that unravels his quiet life. Now he's neck-deep in a conspiracy involving one of the most powerful people in America — while protecting a woman who may know more than she's letting on and the child they didn't plan for.

Devon faces an impossible choice: stay silent and watch America sleepwalk into tyranny, or expose a conspiracy that will make him sound insane — and put him directly in the crosshairs.

For fans of Blake Crouch and Andy Weir.

About the author

Conner Michael is a dad from the Midwest. When he's not writing, he's either spending time with his wife and children, trying not to screw up a new recipe, or enjoying a tall pint with his buddies. Conner started the RAIN series as a way to explore where this new world of AI might actually take us. This is his debut novel.
